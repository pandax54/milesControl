## Recommendations

1. **Consider pagination**: If clients scale to hundreds, consider paginating the matching clients dialog to avoid rendering very large tables.

2. **Cache consideration**: For admins with many clients, `getPromotionsWithClientMatches()` could become expensive. Consider caching the client enrollments list with a TTL if promotions are fetched frequently.

3. **Batch operations**: Task 6.3 (Send targeted recommendations) will likely benefit from the well-structured data this service provides.
